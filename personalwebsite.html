INTRODUCTION :

Organic farming is a way of growing food that focuses on using natural methods to take care of the soil and plants. Instead of using chemicals to protect crops from bugs or help them grow, organic farmers use things like natural fertilizers (like compost), rotating crops, and using helpful insects to keep pests away. They also avoid using genetically modified organisms (GMOs) and synthetic chemicals. Organic farming is good for the environment because it keeps the soil healthy and reduces pollution. Many people choose organic food because they believe it's healthier and better for the planet, even though it can be more challenging and sometimes costs more for farmers to do.
